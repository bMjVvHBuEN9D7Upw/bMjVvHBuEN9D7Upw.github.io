English Essay1 - comtemporary society

發表於2021-12-26|更新於2022-05-20|English

In the comtemporary society,most peopele use social media for news,entertainment,to search information and to be updated every day.
Nowdays,the use of social media has greatly changed how people interact with other people. in fact social media have become a part of humans’lives.

在當代世界，大多數人使用社交媒體獲取新聞、娛樂、尋找信息和每天更新。
如今，社交媒體的使用極大地改變了人們與他人互動的方式。事實上，社交媒體已經成為人類生活的一部分。

First of all, I think social media will affect student performance, whether it is social media or computers. As long as you use it incorrectly and well, it will also affect your own health. However, in today’s society, you see that many brains are underdeveloped. When you see information on social media, you will learn from them and follow them. This can easily cause a problem. Wrong growth. Based on this, I think that when something is too easy to touch, learning is not something that everyone likes. In most cases, you will touch what you want to touch, so you will automatically filter and indulge. In his own world, causing a bad situation.

首先，我認為社交媒體會影響學生的成績，無論是社交媒體，電腦。只要不正確良好的使用也會影響到你自己的健康狀況，但在現今社會上，看到很多腦部未發育，當看到社交媒體上的資料會學習他們和跟隨他們，這樣很容易造成一個錯誤的成長，基於這樣，我認為當一樣東西太容易接觸到的時候，學習不是一樣每個人也喜歡的事情，絕大部分情況都是會接觸你想接觸的東西，因而會自動過濾，並沉迷於他自己的世界，造成一個不良的情況。

Although there are countless advantages, the disadvantages cannot be ignored. On the personal side, social media will make young people waste their studies, because many young people have no self-control ability and will browse different social media for a long time after class. Once there was a young man who was obsessed with making friends on Facebook day and night, and his exam results plummeted. In addition, an agency survey report pointed out that 30% of teenagers continue to surf the Internet during their spare time, which affects their academic performance. It seems that excessive use of social media will really ruin the future of young people.

儘管﹐有無數的好處﹐但是也不能忽略其壞處。在個人方面﹐社交媒體會令青少年荒廢學業﹐因為很多青少年都沒有自制能力﹐一下課便長時間瀏覽不同的社交媒體。曾經有一位青年日以繼夜地沉迷在面書上交朋友﹐令考試的成績一落千丈。此外﹐有機構調查報告指出﹐有三成表少年會在課餘時間不斷上網﹐因而影響學業成績。看來﹐過度使用社交媒體真的會把青少年的前途盡毀。

Secondly, from a personal perspective, social media can broaden the horizons of young people. In the virtual world, they can meet different friends, so that they can learn different aspects of knowledge from different people, such as language, so social media can really broaden the horizons of young people.

另外，從個人方面而言﹐社交媒體能擴濶青少年的視野。在虛擬世界中﹐他們能結識不同的朋友﹐以此可以從不同人身上學到不同方面的知識,例如語言，所以社交媒體的確能擴闊青少年的視野。

Finally, from the social aspect, social media will affect the social and communication skills of young people. Young people have been facing the virtual online world for a long time, completely ignoring the real world. What is the result? They have reduced their contact with friends. In severe cases, they are more likely to develop autism. This is because they are “trapped” by social media and will never be able to escape the “devil’s grasp.” So social media does affect the social and communication skills of young people.

最後﹐從社交方面而言﹐社交媒體會影響青少年的社交和溝通能力。青少年長期對著虛擬的網絡世界﹐完全無視現實世界﹐結果是什麼﹖他們減少了與朋友接觸﹐嚴重的話﹐更有可能患上自閉症﹐這就是因為他們被社交媒體「困」起來﹐永遠也不能逃出「魔掌」。所以社交媒體的確會影響青少年的社交和溝通能力。

All in all, it is like a knife, used properly, it is a tool for cooking; on the contrary, if used improperly, it is a weapon that hurts oneself and others. Therefore, we need to use it properly so that social media can play its true function.

總括而言﹐它就像一把刀﹐用得恰當﹐它就是煮食的工具；相反﹐用得不當﹐它卻是一種傷害自己和別人的武器。因此﹐我們要用得其所﹐社交媒體才能發揮它真正的功用。
